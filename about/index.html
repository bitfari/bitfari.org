Discover all there is to know about Bitfari!

Welcome! Bitfari is a secure and decentralized network for peer-to-peer advertising built using blockchain technology. In Bitfari, users can build community, and collaborate while maintaining their privacy. With Bitfari, you can “Bring Life to Your Location”.

 

If you are new to Bitfari and wish to figure out what it is and how it works, please read the whitepaper here: https://bitfari.org/whitepaper/.

MANTRA
Democratize advertising.

MISSION
Improve the lives of billions by increasing the relevance of business-to-consumer communications and the quality of social connections.

 

VISION
Build the biggest communication network on the planet, surpassing every other medium, spanning billions of nodes, and offering unprecedented value to all touched by Bitfari.
